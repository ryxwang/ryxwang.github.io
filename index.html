I am a Lecturer and DECRA Fellow in the School of Mathematics and Statistics at the University of Sydney. Before joining the school, I received my PhD in Statistics at the University of California, Berkeley and was a Stein Fellow in the Department of Statistics at Stanford University.

My research interests broadly lie in statistical network modelling, statistical machine learning, and their applications to large-scale, complex genomics data.
